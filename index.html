About UKCCU

A community of Christ-like believers growing as a family

Upper Kabete Campus Christian Union (UKCCU) is a campus-based Christian union focused on fellowship, discipleship, leadership development, mentorship, and evangelism — where it happens the kingdom's way.

Our mission is to equip members through these core areas. Our vision is a community of Christ-like believers growing as a family in love for Christ and fellow men.
