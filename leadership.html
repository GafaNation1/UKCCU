UKCCU Leadership Team

Our leadership serves UKCCU through various roles. Below are the key groups that guide our fellowship.

Leadership Structure

UKCCU leadership is organized into distinct groups, each with specific responsibilities and roles in guiding the fellowship:

  • Executive: Core leadership positions responsible for overall direction and decision-making.
  • Subcommittee: Specialized committees supporting various aspects of UKCCU activities.
  • Associates: Supporting members who contribute to specific initiatives and programs.
  • Staff CU: Staff members who provide guidance and support to student leadership.
  • Advisory: Advisory members offering counsel and oversight to the leadership team.
